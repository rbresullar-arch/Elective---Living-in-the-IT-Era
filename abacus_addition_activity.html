Abacus Addition Activity

Numbers appear one at a time. Add each one to your running total on the abacus. The top bead is worth 5 — swipe it down toward the bar to add 5, up to remove it. The lower beads are worth 1 each — swipe up to add, down to subtract. The blue bead is the next one that will move. Rods are independent — a full rod (9) carries over to the next rod when you swipe up again, and the rod above lights up orange. The next number only appears once your total is correct. Once all 5 numbers are done, type the total and check. This repeats for 5 rounds.
